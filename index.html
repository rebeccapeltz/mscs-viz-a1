Average Global Temperature Per Season

Data source: https://data.giss.nasa.gov/gistemp/

The chart below provides seasonal temperature deviations corresponding 1951-1980 means for the years 1880-2015. The data has been bucketed so that deviations are averaged per decade to smooth the lines and make differences more apparent. Below the chart I have provided tabular data for the means computed per decade.

Each line in the chart represents a season. These season means are computed on 3 month periods:

  • Winter = December of Prior Year, January, Februray
  • Spring = March, April, May
  • Summer = June, July August
  • Fall = September, October, November
